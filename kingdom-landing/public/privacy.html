Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Kingdom AI collects NO personal data. Zero. None. This is by design, not by accident.

1. What Data We Collect

None. Kingdom AI does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information, usage data, analytics, telemetry, crash reports, or any other data from your device.

Specifically, Kingdom AI does NOT collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports or error logs
  • Browsing history or search queries
  • Financial data, wallet addresses, or transaction history
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Contacts, call logs, or messages

2. How the App Works

Kingdom AI runs entirely on your device. All processing — including AI inference, trading signals, mining operations, and wallet management — happens locally on your phone or computer.

The app does not communicate with any Kingdom AI servers. There are no Kingdom AI cloud services, backend databases, or remote APIs that receive your data.

3. Network Connections

Kingdom AI may make network connections only for the following purposes, all initiated by you:

  • Cryptocurrency exchanges — When you connect your own exchange API keys (e.g., Kraken, Binance), the app communicates directly with those exchanges. We never see or store your API keys.
  • Mining pools — When you choose to mine, the app connects directly to public mining pools. No data passes through Kingdom AI servers.
  • Local network sync — When you link your phone to your desktop, communication happens over your local Wi-Fi network only. No data leaves your network.
  • AI models — AI runs locally via Ollama on your device. No queries are sent to external AI services.

4. Wallet & Key Security

All cryptocurrency private keys and wallet data are generated and stored exclusively on your device. Kingdom AI has no mechanism to access, view, copy, or transmit your private keys. This is an architectural decision — the code physically cannot exfiltrate keys.

5. Open Source Transparency

Kingdom AI is 100% open source. Every line of code is publicly auditable. You can verify these privacy claims yourself by inspecting the source code. There are no obfuscated binaries, hidden network calls, or encrypted payloads.

6. Third-Party Services

Kingdom AI does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection SDKs. There is no Google Analytics, no Firebase, no Facebook SDK, no AppsFlyer, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude — nothing.

7. Children's Privacy

Kingdom AI is not directed at children under 18. The app involves cryptocurrency trading and mining, which are adult financial activities. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

8. Data Retention

Since we collect no data, there is nothing to retain. All app data (settings, preferences, wallet info) is stored locally on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

9. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations, you have rights regarding your personal data. Since Kingdom AI collects no personal data, these rights are automatically satisfied — there is no data to access, correct, delete, or port.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. Our core commitment will never change: Kingdom AI will never collect your data.
